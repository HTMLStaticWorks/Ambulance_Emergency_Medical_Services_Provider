Saving Lives Since 2010

AmbulancePro was founded with a singular focus: eliminating transit delays during critical medical events. What started as a small local initiative has grown into India's premier emergency network, responding to calls with medical precision and utmost empathy.

Today, we operate in 8 major cities, supported by hundreds of trauma professionals, state-of-the-art telemetry equipment, and a unified emergency tracking system.
